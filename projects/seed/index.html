News

[2 Oct 2023]  We are excited to unveil SEED-LLaMA, which offers unified multimodal comprehension and generation capabilities, featuring multi-turn in-context emergent capabilities, akin to an AI aide. SEED-LLaMA is empowered by the SEED-2 tokenizer with improved visual semantics.

[30 July 2023]  SEED-Bench , the most comprehensive MLLM benchmark, has been released! It consists of 19K multiple-choice questions with accurate human annotations, spans 12 evaluation dimensions in terms of both spatial and temporal comprehension.

  • Links: Tech Report, Code, Data, Leaderboard
  • Features:

[16 July 2023]  We are glad to release the initial version of SEED, a tailored image tokenizer that empowers Large Language Models with the emergent ability to see and draw.
