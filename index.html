I am currently a senior researcher at Applied Research Center (ARC), Tencent PCG in Beijing. I got my Ph.D. degree from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021, supervised by Prof. Hongsheng Li and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. Previously, I received bachelor's degree in Measurement and Control Technology and Instrument at Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2017. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, with focus on large-scale pre-training, un/self-/semi-/weakly-supervised learning, image/video/cross-modality representation learning, transfer learning, feature compatible learning, etc.
