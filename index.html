About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I am supervised by Prof. Hongsheng Li and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. Before that, I received bachelor's degree in Measurement and Control Technology and Instrument at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, with focus on unsupervised learning, disentangled learning, domain adaptation, image retrieval and image generation.
