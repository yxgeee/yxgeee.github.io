I am currently a senior researcher at Tencent ARC Lab (Applied Research Center, PCG) in Beijing. I got my Ph.D. degree from Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Hongsheng Li and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. Previously, I received the B.Eng. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, with focuses on large-scale pre-training, un/self-/semi-/weakly-supervised learning, image/video/cross-modality representation learning, transfer learning, etc.
If you are looking to join Tencent ARC Lab for an internship or as a full-time researcher, feel free to contact me.
